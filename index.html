Welcome to MelodyPoint Music School

MelodyPoint Music School is a premier center for music education located in the heart of Monrovia, Liberia. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced musician, our school provides a warm, professional, and inspiring environment to grow your musical talent.

We offer lessons in guitar, piano, vocals, drums, and more. Our certified instructors bring years of experience and a true passion for music. At MelodyPoint, music is not just a subject -- it is a lifestyle.

Why Choose MelodyPoint?

With over 10 years of service to the Liberian music community, MelodyPoint has trained hundreds of students who have gone on to perform professionally at concerts, churches, and events across the country. Join a community that believes in your musical future.
