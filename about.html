About MelodyPoint Music School

Our Story

MelodyPoint Music School was founded in 2014 by a group of passionate Liberian musicians who believed that every child and adult deserved access to quality music education. Starting with just three instructors and a small rented space in Sinkor, Monrovia, the school has grown into one of the most respected music training centers in Liberia.

Today, MelodyPoint serves over 200 active students per term and employs a team of certified music instructors. Our alumni have performed at the Executive Mansion, churches, international festivals, and on national television.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality music education to students of all ages and backgrounds, nurturing creativity, discipline, and a lifelong love for music in the Liberian community and beyond.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We hold our instructors and students to the highest standards of musical skill and professionalism.

Inclusivity

Music is for everyone. We welcome students regardless of age, background, or prior experience.

Creativity

We encourage students to explore their own musical voice and express themselves freely.

Discipline

Great music requires consistent practice. We teach the value of dedication and routine.

Community

We build a family of musicians who support, inspire, and perform together.

Achievement

We celebrate every milestone -- from the first chord to the stage performance.

Accreditation and Affiliations

MelodyPoint is recognized by the Liberia National Cultural Association and is an affiliate member of the West Africa Music Education Network (WAMEN). Our curriculum follows internationally accepted music education standards.
