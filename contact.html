Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you accept?

MelodyPoint accepts students from age 5 years and above. We have programs specially designed for children, teenagers, and adults.

Do I need to own an instrument to enroll?

No. We provide practice instruments during class. However, students are encouraged to practice at home and we can advise you on purchasing a suitable beginner instrument.

How long is each term?

Each term at MelodyPoint runs for 12 weeks. We offer three terms per year. Students may enroll at the start of any term.

Can I try a class before enrolling?

Yes. We offer a free trial lesson for all new students. Contact us to schedule your trial session.
