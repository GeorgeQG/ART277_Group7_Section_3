Our Programs and Gallery

MelodyPoint Music School offers a wide range of music programs designed for beginners, intermediate, and advanced students. Explore our offerings and see what our students have been creating.

Music Programs Offered

We offer the following programs across all age groups and skill levels:

  • Piano Lessons -- Classical, gospel, contemporary, and jazz piano for all levels
  • Guitar Lessons -- Acoustic, electric, and bass guitar with theory included
  • Vocal Training -- Singing techniques, pitch, tone, and stage performance
  • Drumming and Percussion -- Drumset, congas, djembe, and African rhythms
  • Music Theory -- Reading sheet music, notation, scales, and harmony
  • Music Production -- Introduction to digital audio workstations (DAWs)

Enrollment Requirements

To enroll at MelodyPoint Music School, students must provide the following:

  1. Completed enrollment form (available at our front desk or on request)
  2. One passport-sized photo
  3. Copy of school ID or national ID (for students age 16 and above)
  4. First month tuition payment at time of registration
  5. Parent or guardian consent form (for students under 18)
